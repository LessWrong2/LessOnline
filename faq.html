Whose writings is this event about?

This event is for those who write fiction or non-fiction inspired (in part) by an aspiration to rationality.

  • Blogs like Astral Codex Ten, Don't Worry About the Vase, PutANumOnIt, Minding Our Way, Gwern, and more, along with the community blog LessWrong.
  • Stories like Harry Potter and the Methods of Rationality, UNSONG, Worth the Candle, Luminosity, Planecrash, and more.

What sorts of things will happen at LessOnline?

  • Double Cruxes: Pairs of people with substantive disagreements will work together to find the crux of their disagreements â€” a subquestion that would change both of their minds.
  • Storytelling: Authors will read new stories that they have written.
  • Podcasts: Some typical rational podcasts will have a live recording.
  • Workshops: Some former CFAR Instructors will give classes on tools like Murphyjitsu and Trigger-Action Plans.
  • Open mic: Ricki Heicklen will MC a talent show / open mic / good-natured opportunity to roast the community.
  • Memo Sessions: Writers will hand out printed copies of draft blogposts for small group discussions with the writer.

Over the weekend, there will be a continuously updating collaborative schedule for sessions that anyone is invited to add to.

Who is going to be there?

Lots of bloggers and fiction authors, plus some Manifold Summer Camp attendees, and however many hundreds of people apply for a ticket that we can fit. My guess is 400 people.

And hopefully you!

Who is organizing the event?

Ben Pace and Ricki Heicklen, from Lightcone Infrastructure.

How much is a ticket?

$400, minus your LessWrong karma in cents (minimum $0). This includes breakfast, lunch and dinner. You optionally can buy a Lightweight ticket at $275 that doesn't cover food. We also offer bedrooms and childcare as add-ons.

Can I bring my kids?

Absolutely!

We can support people living on-site with their children and we will have paid daycare available for the duration of the event.
