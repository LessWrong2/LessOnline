Lighthaven

Lighthaven is a campus located in Berkeley, California.

It’s run by Lightcone Infrastructure, and we hope to use this place to make the future of humanity go better than it would go otherwise.

What Happens Here?

Team Retreats

15 - 70 people

We offer cozy nooks with firepits, discussion rooms with endless whiteboards, and up to 46 bedrooms (with up to 70 beds).

Parties & Events

10 - 500 people

From private dinners to 500 person parties. Sound setup, music, private kitchens, snacks, catering.

Conferences

50 - 600 people

Conference space for up to 600 people. 20+ session spaces. 70 conference attendees can book accommodation on the campus.

Pricing

Retreats and Conferences:
$100 - $400 per person per day.

Parties:
$25 - $100 per person.

We offer some groups large discounts if we think a project seems particularly great to us. Please feel quite free to ask us about this.

You can use your own caterer, but our default caterer offers meals from $20/meal to $50/meal that most visitors have found quite satisfying.

We engage in some price discrimination – we try to find a fair price that splits the difference between our own costs and the value you get out of the space. Overall we try to be reasonable about pricing, and don't expect to make much profit on the space.

Services We Provide

We can provide (among many other things):

  • Catering services, snacks & drinks
  • General ops-support for your event
  • Event branding and marketing

We have a lot of experience running all kinds of different events, from small 15-20 person dinners to 1000+ person conferences, to 2-month fellowships and research programs.

We can’t guarantee we can provide everything you need, but if you are facing some kind of problem or obstacle in the course of preparing or running your event, we can probably help you.
