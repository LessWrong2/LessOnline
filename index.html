What's Lighthaven?

Lighthaven is a campus located in Berkeley, California. It’s run by Lightcone Infrastructure, and we hope to use this place to make the future of humanity go better than it would go otherwise.

Book an Event

What Happens Here?

Our campus consists of 7 buildings, including two retreat centers that you can book for money (and for weekend conferences you can rent out the whole campus). Sometimes you can rent them for less money or for free because we think your project or event might make the world better. The space is huge and very modular, and can accommodate a lot of different types of events.

Team Retreats

15 - 70 people

Office space, endless whiteboards, up to 46 bedrooms (with up to 70 beds), catering, supplies.

Parties & Events

10 - 500 people

Event space ranging from private dinners to 500 person parties. Sound setup, music, private kitchens, snacks, catering.

Conferences

50 - 600 people

Conference space for up to 600 people. 20+ session spaces. 70 conference attendees can book accommodation on the campus.

Services We Provide

We can provide (among many other things):

  • Catering services, snacks & drinks
  • General ops-support for your event
  • Event branding and marketing

We have a lot of experience running all kinds of different events, from small 15-20 person dinners to 1000+ person conferences, to 2-month fellowships and research programs.

We can’t guarantee we can provide everything you need, but if you are facing some kind of problem or obstacle in the course of preparing or running your event, we can probably help you.
