What is LessOnline?

31 May - 2 June, 2024

Lighthaven Campus, Berkeley, CA

LessOnline is an in-person festival celebrating truth-seeking, optimization, and blogging. It's an an opportunity to meet people you've only ever known by their LessWrong username or pseudonymous Substack handle.

The goal is to bring together a "mostly-online subculture of people trying to work together to figure out how to distinguish truth from falsehood using insights from probability theory, cognitive science, and AI." [1]

The structure will be determined by you: We'll have a collaborative schedule for "unconference" sessions, where attendees can sign up to run anything they want. If you have a session or activity you might like to run at LessOnline, let us know!
